about the artist and scholar

Hi my name is Maggie Washburn, welcome to my creative portfolio! Thank you for being here. A bit about me, my favorite number is three, my favorite color is yellow and my favorite sports team is the University of Connecticut Women’s Basketball. I am a graduating senior at the College of Charleston with a Computing in the Arts Major with a Digital Media Concentration and a Studio Arts Minor. I am hoping to find a job that fulfills my creative needs, and want to make sure employers get a good idea of who I am and what I bring to the table. That being said, I coded this entire site to showcase some of my favorite things that I’ve made. Please travel through and let me know your thoughts.

I have always been consumed by all media and channels of art. Music, architecture, live events, exhibits, collaging, talent scoping, collaborating, producing—any part of the creative world can contribute to. I fit into corporate America in a very unique way. Every company needs new ideas and creative drive, and here I am ready to become a part of one of these companies. I want to help visions grow and come to life, storyboard new concepts, brainstorm products, and figure out how to get those products to the audience that didn’t know they needed them. I live to create, and I want to create to live.

This is a very high-level overview of my project list. Please reach out if you want to see more of my work or if you have any comments.
