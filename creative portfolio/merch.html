While most people think of paint and charcoal when they hear art, my brain immediately goes to doodling. There’s nothing like the power of a good doodle! I used to design and sew my own clothes. As I got older, I found I could bulk create merchandise and made my first t-shirt for Camp Harrison, a sleepaway camp I attended and worked at. When transitioning to college, I made a sweatshirt for my sorority, which caught lots of eyes. I also made merchandise for Volume Three of INSCAPE, all of the profit was sent to various women’s charities. The North Carolina State Women’s Field Hockey Team reached out and asked me to make merchandise for their 2025/206 season! They were pleased enough with my work that I got asked back to make merchandise for their upcoming 2026/2027 season, and I am beyond excited to work with them again.
